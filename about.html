About Luna Vogue Boutique

Our History

Founded in 2019 in Colombo, Luna Vogue Boutique began as a small studio with a passion for handcrafted, contemporary apparel. We started with a small capsule of artisanal pieces and have grown into a carefully curated boutique that celebrates style and sustainability.

Vision

To be the go-to boutique for modern, sustainable fashion that empowers people to express themselves with confidence.

Mission

  • Design and offer high-quality garments with a timeless aesthetic.
  • Practice sustainable sourcing and responsible production.
  • Create an inclusive shopping experience, online and in-store.

Values

Creativity • Sustainability • Quality • Customer-centricity
